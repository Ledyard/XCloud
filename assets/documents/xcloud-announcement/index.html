XCloud Extension 0.3.0 Release

Everyone rejoice, we have a new XCloud up and running!
(Hopefully it won't immediately buckle under the load...)

What this means to you.

For users on new XKit and couldn't get XCloud to work

You can now use XCloud to backup and restore your user settings.

For users on new XKit and was using XCloud perfectly fine

XCloud will still work for you. After you update you still should be able to just keep using XCloud normally.
If XCloud stops working after the update, you may need to login again.

Just make sure you check "Use the old XCloud Servers" on the welcome page. to use the old XCloud servers.


If you want to migrate to the new server you can logout of XCloud and register for a new account.
Then just sync and you're on the new XCloud.

For users on old XKit who want to upgrade but cannot upgrade through the old XCloud

We have a migration guide which will use XCloud to store your information and then restore your settings after you upgrade to New-XKit.
